About Little Minds

A calm, beautiful space built for curious little minds.

Little Minds Montessori is more than childcare – it is a thoughtfully prepared environment where children are respected as capable, curious individuals. Our classrooms are designed with soft, natural tones and open shelves so children can move, explore, and choose purposeful work.

Guided by Montessori philosophy, our educators observe each child closely, offer gentle guidance, and follow their natural rhythm. We prioritise emotional safety, independence, and joyful discovery over busy, noisy chaos.

  • Montessori-inspired materials and child‑sized environments
  • Low teacher–student ratios for deep, individual attention
  • Structured routines with plenty of outdoor and free‑play time
  • Secure, hygienic campus with safety‑first policies

Why parents choose Little Minds

Programs

Montessori programs for each stage of early childhood.

Infant Program

6 – 18 months

A tender, peaceful space focused on bonding, sensory exploration, and secure attachment. Infants follow their own sleep and feeding rhythm while gently introduced to Montessori materials.

  • Individualised feeding and sleep routines
  • Soft, safe floor time for free movement
  • Sensory baskets, textures, and music

Toddler Program

18 months – 3 years

A joyful, hands‑on environment where toddlers practice independence, language, and practical life skills – pouring, cleaning, dressing, and caring for their classroom.

  • Practical life and sensorial activities
  • Language‑rich songs, stories, and conversations
  • Support with toileting and self‑care routines

Pre‑K Montessori

3 – 5 years

A fully prepared Montessori environment supporting early literacy, numeracy, problem‑solving, and social‑emotional skills – all through purposeful, self‑directed work.

  • Montessori language and math materials
  • Grace and courtesy lessons
  • Outdoor play and nature exploration
